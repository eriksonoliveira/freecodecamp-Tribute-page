Isaac Newton

One of the greatests scientists of all time

Here's a time line of Isaac Newton's life:

  • 1642 - Born in Loncolnshire, England
  • 1661 - Enrolled in a study program at the University of Cambridge's Trinity College
  • 1667 - Received his Master of Arts degree
  • 1668 - Deseigns ad builds a reflecting telescope
  • 1687 - Published his most acclaimed work: Mathematical Principles of Natural Philosophy - Principia
  • 1689 - Elected to represent Cambridge in Parliament
  • 1705 - Becomes the first British scientist to be knighted
  • 1727 - Newton dies at the age of 84
